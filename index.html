A creative journey turned into a studio

XoRdbX Studio was born from the desire to combine programming, design, and gameplay into meaningful interactive experiences.

What is XoRdbX?

XoRdbX is an independent game studio founded in the Valenciennes area of northern France. The project started as a personal creative endeavor — a way to explore the world of mobile game development through a combination of artistic sensibility and technical passion.

From the very first lines of code to publishing on the Google Play Store, every aspect of the studio's first game was conceived, designed, and built by a single developer. This solo-developed approach means that each creative decision reflects a genuine personal vision rather than the compromises of a larger team.

XoRdbX is registered as a sole proprietorship (EI) with the Valenciennes Trade and Companies Register, demonstrating a commitment to operating professionally and transparently within the French legal framework.

Philosophy & Vision

The studio's philosophy centers on one key principle: accessible fun. Games should be easy to pick up but rewarding to master. LexiGrid embodies this through intuitive touch controls, clean visual design, and progressively challenging levels.

XoRdbX believes that word games are more than entertainment — they stimulate the mind, expand vocabulary, and sharpen cognitive skills. By combining this educational dimension with genuine fun and appealing aesthetics, the studio aims to create games that players return to day after day.

Future projects will continue to explore the intersection of puzzle mechanics, language, and elegant design. The catalog will grow, always guided by the same standards of quality and clarity that define LexiGrid.

The first game released by XoRdbX Studio — a word puzzle experience built around speed, strategy, and the love of language.

What is LexiGrid?

LexiGrid – Letter Swap Quest is a fast-paced word puzzle game for Android. The core mechanic is elegantly simple: players swap adjacent letters on a dynamic 10×10 grid to form valid words before the clock runs out.

Each word you complete rewards you with bonus time, keeping the tension alive and encouraging you to push further. The game progressively increases in complexity — new grid layouts, tighter time limits, and special obstacle tiles like slime and ice keep every session fresh and stimulating.

LexiGrid is designed to be picked up in seconds but mastered over dozens of sessions. Whether you have two minutes on the commute or an hour at home, the game adapts to your rhythm and rewards your vocabulary skills.

Beyond entertainment, LexiGrid actively trains your brain. Searching for words under time pressure sharpens reaction speed, improves pattern recognition, and enriches your vocabulary — all while having fun.

C'est quoi LexiGrid ?

LexiGrid – Letter Swap Quest est un jeu de puzzle de mots au rythme soutenu, disponible sur Android. La mécanique principale est d'une simplicité élégante : les joueurs échangent des lettres adjacentes sur une grille dynamique de 10×10 cases pour former des mots valides avant que le temps ne s'écoule.

Chaque mot complété vous rapporte du temps supplémentaire, maintenant la tension et vous encourageant à aller encore plus loin. Le jeu gagne progressivement en complexité — nouvelles dispositions de grilles, temps plus serrés, et tuiles obstacles spéciales comme le slime et la glace — pour que chaque session reste fraîche et stimulante.

LexiGrid se prend en main en quelques secondes mais se maîtrise au fil de dizaines de parties. Que vous ayez deux minutes dans les transports ou une heure chez vous, le jeu s'adapte à votre rythme et récompense vos compétences linguistiques.

Au-delà du divertissement, LexiGrid entraîne activement votre cerveau. Chercher des mots sous la pression du temps affûte la vitesse de réaction, améliore la reconnaissance de schémas et enrichit le vocabulaire — tout en s'amusant.

LexiGrid

Core game highlights

Grid-based word gameplay

Create words directly on the board by swapping adjacent letters. Scan the grid, spot opportunities, and build the longest words you can to maximize your bonus time. The larger the word, the bigger the reward.

Quick puzzle sessions

Short, dynamic rounds designed for mobile play — perfect for commutes, breaks, or any spare moment. Sessions are intense enough to be satisfying and short enough to always leave you wanting one more round.

Strategic obstacles

Slime tiles block letter swaps and must be cleared strategically. Ice tiles freeze letters in place until you work around them. These mechanics turn each level into a genuine puzzle that rewards careful planning alongside quick thinking.

Progressive difficulty

Each stage introduces new layouts, configurations, and challenges. Early levels ease you in, while later stages demand both a rich vocabulary and sharp strategic thinking to complete before time expires.

Brain training

LexiGrid actively trains vocabulary, pattern recognition, and decision-making under pressure. Regular play helps expand your word bank and sharpens the mental agility needed to spot words in complex, shifting letter arrangements.

Clean, accessible design

The visual design prioritizes clarity and readability. Large, legible letters, high-contrast tile states, and a clean color palette mean you always know exactly what is on the board and what actions are available.
