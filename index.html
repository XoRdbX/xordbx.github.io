What is XoRdbX?

XoRdbX is an independent game studio born from a creative hobby. The project began as a way to explore game development while combining artistic expression and programming. Over time, it evolved into a small studio dedicated to building accessible and engaging games.

Its first title, LexiGrid – Letter Swap Quest, reflects this philosophy through clear gameplay mechanics and thoughtful design.

Games

Current project

LexiGrid is the first game released by XoRdbX Studio. It is a word puzzle game built around a dynamic grid where players swap letters to form words under time pressure.

LexiGrid

LexiGrid – Letter Swap Quest is a fast-paced word puzzle game for Android. Players swap letters on a 10×10 grid to create words before time runs out. Each stage introduces new layouts and challenges, encouraging quick thinking and strategic choices.

Obstacles such as slime and ice tiles add another layer of puzzle-solving, while each successful word rewards players with additional time to keep the game going. Designed to stimulate vocabulary and cognitive skills, LexiGrid combines learning and entertainment in a visually clean and accessible experience.
